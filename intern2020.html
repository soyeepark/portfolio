Apple UX Design Internship

UX Design Intern • 12 Weeks Summer 2020

Overview

During summer 2020, I worked as a UX design intern on the Claris team at Apple. I was part of the Claris connect team, designing for intelligent workflow automation.

I cannot disclose my work (under NDA) but my tasks included the following:

  • Work closely with senior designs on variety of design topics
  • Independently drive concepts creation, wireframes, high fidelity mockups, visual design and prototypes based on product requirements
  • Discuss designs with PM teams and deliver design documentation to developers
  • Give and receive design critique on a continuous basis
