PayPal

UX Design Intern / Summer 2020

During my summer 2021, I worked as a UX design intern on the PayPal UI Design System team at PayPal. My work is under NDA but my work consists of the following resonsibilities:

  • Work on PayPal’s Design System team to help create scalable, accessible design patterns for PayPal product experiences worldwide

  • “Be the customer” and work towards creating experiences customers will love

  • Help create scalable designs for various customer segments, on a variety of form factors, and regions globally

  • Work within existing style guides and patterns, while continuing to push the envelope for better experiences for our customers

  • Use divergent and convergent thinking to explore many possible design solutions

  • Provide design concepts that align to project goals

  • Clearly articulate design decisions and confidently speak about the rationale for design solutions

  • Use design tools to help present designs to key stakeholders across all levels and disciplines

  • Facilitate design discussions with clear and high-quality outputs
