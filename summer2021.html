PayPal

UX Design Intern / Summer 2021

During my summer 2021, I worked as a UX design intern on the PayPal UI Design System team at PayPal. At this time, my work is under NDA.
Check out PayPal's Digital Wallet 2.0 🤑🌮💰🍟🚀 Here's an overview of what I did during my time at PayPal:
  • Define haptic guidelines for PayPal UI’s design system across PayPal brands to optimize for accessibility, core experience and moments of delight for the in-app experience
  • Audit and align on micro-interactions and visual language on the web hero currency input component to create a scalable, consistent, and transparent design system
Takeaways
  1. Be an active communicator and a cross-functional partner. Be a channel that brings different domain teams together to create alignment. Bring these concerns early because new components tend to be contagious.
  2. Learn to work with ambiguity and limited research. Cross-functional partners sometime don't have the bandwidth to optimize and provide the perfect insights. Ask questions actively but also look for ways to do your own research.
  3. Never trade off consistency over creative freedom during the exploratory phase. Don't be scared to break the design system.
