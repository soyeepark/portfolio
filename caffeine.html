Caffeine App for Apple Watch

Product Design • Side Project • 1 week October 2020

Overview

In this project, I designed the end-to-end coffee caffeine tracker app for Apple Watch. After research I found that people find it difficult to break bad coffee consumption habits. I designed a solution that provides users healthy alternatives to disencourage excess caffeine consumption.

High-level Problem

In a study funded by the National Institute on Drug Abuse, 44% of people with caffeine dependence continued consumption despite harm and only 23% expressed desire to cut down or control use.

Source: Journal of Caffeine Research: Evaluating Dependence Criteria for Caffeine

Research

Background Research: Risks of excess caffeine intake
  • Liver damage
  • Increase blood pressure
  • Headache
  • Anxiety
  • Insomnia
User Research

I interviewed people who drink Coffee on a day to day basis understand their pain-points and discover the problem. My key insights are the following:

  1. People are unaware of the recommended caffeine intake
    “When I’m drinking coffee, I don’t realize I am drinking too much and I’m not sure how much is recommended”

  2. People don't feel the need to stop drinking coffee until they experience a health problem
    "I don't feel the need to stop drinking coffee until I experience a headache”

  3. People who drink too much coffee drink it as a habit
    “I drink coffee because its just become part of my work routine.”
Competitive Analysis

I took a look at other caffeine tracking apps to identify strengths and weaknesses on how they are solving the problem.

Define

Problem Statement:
People want to drink coffee without health risks but they can't do that because they are unaware of their recommended caffeine intake and are unmotivated to break consumption habits
Target Audience:
Coffee lovers who care about their health and wellness but struggle because of excess caffeine consumption.

Ideation

Brain storming: How might we help users limit excess caffeine intake?
Problem Solution: Caffeine tracker app that suggests other healthy alternatives when consumed over the targeted limit
  • Why wearable technology? Wearable tech brings convenience, real-time tracking, and efficiency.
